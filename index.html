Climb Color - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Introduction

Climb Color ("we", "our", or "the app") is a puzzle game developed by Ali Budak. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Authentication Data: If you create an account, we collect your email address through Firebase Authentication to enable features such as friend invites and progress syncing.

Game Data: We store your game progress (current level, settings) locally on your device and optionally in Firebase Firestore for syncing purposes.

Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads. For more details, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Tracking: With your permission, we may use Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework to access your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve personalized ads. You can opt out at any time through your device settings.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the game experience
  • Save your game progress
  • Enable social features (friend invites)
  • Display advertisements to support free gameplay

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication and data storage
  • Google AdMob - Advertising

Data Retention

Your game data is stored locally on your device and in Firebase for as long as you use the app. You can delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
